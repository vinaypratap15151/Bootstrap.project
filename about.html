    RULES OF ELITE LIBRARY

    There are certain amount of rules that everyone is supposed to follow at elite library and they are listed below.

    2. each candidate will be given separate cabin and they must maintain decorum of a library
    3. If candidate issues a book then he or she will have to return the book under one month otherwise will have to pay panalty of Rs 10 for each delayed day.
